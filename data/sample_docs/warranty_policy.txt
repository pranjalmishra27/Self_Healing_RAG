SECTION 1: LIMITED WARRANTY COVERAGE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

ACME Corporation ("ACME") warrants the ProDevice X1 ("Product") against defects
in materials and workmanship for a period of TWO (2) YEARS from the original
date of purchase by the end-user purchaser ("Warranty Period").

This warranty covers:
  • Hardware defects in the main unit, battery, and included accessories
  • Screen defects present at the time of purchase
  • Manufacturing defects causing the device to fail to function as intended
  • Internal component failures not caused by user misuse

This warranty does NOT cover:
  • Physical damage, drops, or impacts
  • Liquid damage or exposure to moisture
  • Damage caused by unauthorized modifications or repairs
  • Normal wear and tear, including battery capacity degradation after 500 charge cycles
  • Cosmetic damage such as scratches or dents that do not affect functionality
  • Software issues, viruses, or data loss

SECTION 2: HOW TO OBTAIN WARRANTY SERVICE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

To obtain warranty service, customers must:
  1. Contact ACME Support at support@acme.com or 1-800-ACME-PRO
  2. Provide proof of purchase (receipt or order confirmation)
  3. Describe the defect in detail
  4. Receive a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number
  5. Ship the product to the ACME service center with the RMA number clearly labeled

ACME will, at its sole discretion, repair or replace the defective product at
no charge. Replacement products may be new or refurbished. Shipping of the
repaired/replaced unit back to the customer is covered by ACME.

SECTION 3: EXTENDED WARRANTY — ACME PROTECT+
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

ACME offers an optional extended warranty program called ACME Protect+:

  Basic Plan ($49/year): Extends standard warranty by 1 additional year (total 3 years)
  Plus Plan ($89/year): Extends warranty by 2 years AND adds accidental damage coverage
  Premium Plan ($129/year): All Plus benefits + priority 24-hour support + free annual check-up

ACME Protect+ must be purchased within 30 days of the original product purchase.
Accidental damage coverage includes screen replacements ($29 deductible) and
water damage repairs ($49 deductible).
