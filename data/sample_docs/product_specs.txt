TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

DISPLAY
  Type: 6.7-inch AMOLED, 120Hz adaptive refresh rate
  Resolution: 2800 × 1260 pixels (460 PPI)
  Brightness: 1200 nits peak, 2000 nits HDR peak
  Protection: Corning Gorilla Glass Victus 2

PROCESSOR
  Chipset: ACME Orion A17 (4nm process)
  CPU: 8-core (4× performance + 4× efficiency), up to 3.8 GHz
  GPU: ACME AX Graphics 6-core
  Neural Engine: 16-core, 35 TOPS

MEMORY & STORAGE
  RAM: 8 GB (base) / 12 GB (Pro model)
  Storage: 128 GB / 256 GB / 512 GB (UFS 3.1)
  Expandable storage: Not supported

CAMERA SYSTEM
  Main camera: 50 MP, f/1.8, OIS, 4K/60fps video
  Ultra-wide: 12 MP, f/2.2, 120° field of view
  Telephoto: 10 MP, f/2.9, 5× optical zoom
  Front camera: 12 MP, f/2.2, Face ID support
  Video: Up to 8K/24fps (main camera only)

BATTERY
  Capacity: 4800 mAh
  Charging: 67W wired fast charging (0–100% in 38 minutes)
  Wireless: 15W Qi2 wireless charging
  Reverse wireless: 5W
  Battery life: Up to 26 hours video playback (rated)

CONNECTIVITY
  5G: Sub-6GHz and mmWave
  Wi-Fi: 802.11 a/b/g/n/ac/ax (Wi-Fi 6E)
  Bluetooth: 5.3
  NFC: Yes
  USB: USB-C 3.2 Gen 2 (10 Gbps, DisplayPort 2.0 support)
  SIM: Nano-SIM + eSIM

DIMENSIONS & WEIGHT
  Height: 160.7 mm
  Width: 77.6 mm
  Depth: 8.3 mm
  Weight: 198 g

OPERATING SYSTEM
  Ships with: ACME OS 17
  Guaranteed updates: 4 years of OS updates, 5 years of security patches

SENSORS
  Face ID, barometer, gyroscope, accelerometer, proximity sensor,
  ambient light sensor, magnetometer, UWB chip

AUDIO
  Stereo speakers with Dolby Atmos support
  3.5mm headphone jack: Not included
  Bluetooth audio: aptX HD, LDAC, AAC support

COLORS
  Midnight Black, Arctic White, Ocean Blue, Sunset Gold

IN THE BOX
  • ProDevice X1 unit
  • USB-C to USB-C cable (1m, 67W rated)
  • 67W power adapter
  • SIM ejection tool
  • Quick start guide
  • Warranty documentation

INITIAL SETUP
1. Insert your SIM card using the provided ejection tool.
2. Press and hold the power button for 3 seconds to turn on.
3. Follow the on-screen setup wizard.
4. Sign in with your ACME account or create a new one.
5. Enable Face ID for secure authentication.

TRANSFERRING DATA FROM OLD DEVICE
  - Use the ACME Switch app (iOS/Android) for wireless transfer
  - Or connect via USB-C cable for wired transfer
  - iCloud and Google account import is supported

CHARGING
  - Use only the included 67W adapter for fastest charging
  - The device supports charging up to 20W from third-party Qi chargers
  - Avoid charging in extreme temperatures (above 35°C or below 0°C)

ACME offers a 30-day return policy for devices purchased directly from ACME or
authorised retailers:

  • Device must be in original, undamaged condition with all accessories included
  • Return shipping is free for defective units; customer pays for change-of-mind returns
  • Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days of receiving the return
  • Exchanges for a different model are subject to availability

For devices purchased from third-party retailers, the retailer's own return
policy applies. ACME cannot process returns for third-party purchases.
