ORDERS & SHIPPING
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Q: How long does standard shipping take?
A: Standard shipping takes 3–5 business days within the continental US.
   Express shipping (1–2 business days) is available for an additional $14.99.
   Same-day delivery is available in select cities (New York, Los Angeles,
   Chicago, San Francisco, Seattle) for orders placed before 12 PM local time.

Q: Do you offer free shipping?
A: Yes. Orders over $75 qualify for free standard shipping. ACME Premier
   members receive free express shipping on all orders.

Q: Can I track my order?
A: Yes. You will receive a tracking email within 24 hours of shipment.
   You can also track orders at acme.com/orders using your order number.

Q: Do you ship internationally?
A: ACME ships to 42 countries. International shipping rates vary by destination
   and are calculated at checkout. Most international orders arrive in 7–14
   business days. Import duties and taxes are the responsibility of the recipient.

PAYMENT & PRICING
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Q: What payment methods are accepted?
A: ACME accepts Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover, PayPal,
   Apple Pay, Google Pay, and ACME Gift Cards.

Q: Does ACME offer financing?
A: Yes. ACME partners with Affirm to offer 0% APR financing for 12 months
   on purchases over $200 (subject to credit approval). 24-month financing
   is available at 9.99% APR.

Q: Are prices shown inclusive of tax?
A: Prices shown on the website are before tax. Sales tax is calculated based
   on your shipping address and displayed at checkout.

Q: Is there a student or educator discount?
A: Yes. Verified students and educators receive a 15% discount through
   ACME Education. Verification is done through SheerID. The discount
   applies to most products excluding already-discounted bundles.

ACCOUNT & PRIVACY
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Q: How do I reset my password?
A: Go to acme.com/login and click "Forgot Password". Enter your email address
   and you will receive a reset link within 5 minutes.

Q: Can I delete my ACME account?
A: Yes. Go to Account Settings > Privacy > Delete Account. Account deletion
   is permanent and removes all personal data within 30 days in compliance
   with GDPR and CCPA regulations. Active subscriptions must be cancelled first.

Q: Does ACME sell my personal data?
A: No. ACME does not sell personal data to third parties. Data is used only
   for order fulfilment, product improvement, and personalised recommendations
   (which you can opt out of in your privacy settings).

TECHNICAL SUPPORT
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Q: How do I perform a factory reset on my ProDevice X1?
A: Go to Settings > General > Reset > Erase All Content and Settings.
   You will be prompted for your Face ID or PIN before the reset proceeds.
   Ensure you back up your data first using ACME Cloud or a manual backup.

Q: My device is not charging. What should I do?
A: First, try a different cable and power adapter. If the issue persists,
   clean the USB-C port gently with a dry toothbrush. If still not charging,
   restart the device (hold power + volume down for 10 seconds). If none of
   these work, contact ACME Support for warranty assessment.

Q: How do I check if my device is still under warranty?
A: Visit acme.com/warranty and enter your device serial number (found in
   Settings > About > Serial Number). The page will show your warranty
   expiration date and available service options.

Q: What is ACME Premier?
A: ACME Premier is a subscription membership at $9.99/month or $89/year.
   Benefits include:
     • Free express shipping on all orders
     • Early access to new product launches (48 hours before public)
     • 10% discount on accessories and peripherals
     • Priority customer support with dedicated phone line
     • Exclusive members-only deals and flash sales

Q: Can I cancel ACME Premier at any time?
A: Yes. You can cancel anytime from Account Settings > Subscriptions.
   If you cancel within 30 days of subscribing, you receive a full refund.
   After 30 days, your membership continues until the end of the billing period.
